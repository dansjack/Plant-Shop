About Us

Hey folks! We're Dan and Janey Jack. Janey comes from a background in technical and copywriting, and civil engineering. Dan is a retired Marine with experience in electrical repair and government administration. We might seem like an odd fit for the owners of a small business, but growing and curating our small living space with beautiful plants has been a passion of ours for years, and now we think it's time to share our love of plants with the community!


As our plant collection has continued to grow, our friends and family often ask what plants are right for them and their space? We enjoyed giving our advice to enthusiastic prospective plant-parents and determined that we had a real knack for it! And so, we opened up a storefront where we can present our favorite plants for sale and offer up our cumulative expertise.


FAQ

Can you let me know when you have new cuttings in stock?

Sure thing! Just fill out the contact form and make sure to click the mailing list checkbox.

How often do new clippings become available?

Unfortunately, this is difficult to predict as changes to supply/demand as well as inclement weather, droughts, etc all effect what our overseas partners have available.

Can you deliver a plant to my work/home?

At this time we do not deliver, but we plan to offer local delivery (within 3 miles of Plant Shop) by the end of the year!

Do you have any general tips to keep my plants happy and healthy?

We sure do! Here's a small sample:

  • Not all plants are created equal! A location for one plant may not work for others, so do your research
  • If you have trouble overwatering your plants, try using a water mister instead
  • Rotate your plants a half-turn once a week to promote even growth
