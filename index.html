Welcome!

Plant Shop is here to serve up really neat tropical plants to the Seattle area. We are a small but ambitious start-up looking to bring you quality plants from Southeast Asia and South America. With our growing connections to dedicated vendors (and plant enthusiasts!) across these regions, we look forward to showcasing the best they have to offer.


Visit the plants' page to see what we currently have available. Prices change often, so contact us or drop by to find out how much our plants are going for today! Follow us on Instagram or drop us a line in the contact form to learn more about our stock or get on a waiting list.
